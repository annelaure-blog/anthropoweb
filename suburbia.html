"Objects of Suburbia"

This "moving photography" experimental project was meant to show objects left behind by commuters during the day in the suburbs and rural areas, close to where I live. I wanted to illustrate both materiality of those objects left behind, and "the absence of city" that make those particular landscapes.

Video have been compressed using Handbrake to limit the weight of this website.
